UGA's Future Football Outlook



UGA in recent years has gone through a bit of a dry spell. In fact, as of last year it became so bad that the athletic director

believed that we needed a new head coach all together. This past season, we were able to pick up the highly successful Alabama

defensive coordinator and former UGA football player, Kirby Smart. While this current season we have had struggles facing lesser

teams, it is important for us to remember that even Nick Saban, one of the most successful college coaches in this era, started his

first season with Alabama with a 7-6 record. This isn't even begining to mention the fact that he is also working with a true freshman

quaterback and primary wide receiver. Given our record and the tallent that Smart is working with, I think we are on the road to a

BSC championship within the next 5 years.

Reasons UGA Will Win a Championship Within the Next 5 Years

  • Kirby Smart is an excellent recruiter.
    1. Kirby is still dealing with a Mark Richt recruited team.
    2. Once he begins to recruit his own team our preformance will improve.
  • Kirby knows the ins and outs of Alabama's defense.
    1. The winner of the SEC West is almost always Alabama.
    2. The winner of the SEC championship (west v. east) always goes to the BCS Bowl.
    3. Knowing how to beat Alabama almost guarentees a trip to the BCS Bowl.
  • All other non-SEC schools are trash and won't stand a chance against a real football team.
